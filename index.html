Our Story

ITALIAN SIMPLICITY MEETS NAMMA OORU WARMTH.

Born in a home kitchen, Ni Tinnu — Kannada for “you eat” — brings together the soulful simplicity of Italian sourdough pizza with the warmth of a Bengaluru adda.

In Italy, food is an expression of love. In Kannada, “ni tinnu” is a warm invitation to eat. What began as a homegrown cloud kitchen now brings hand-crafted sourdough pizza — from home deliveries and cozy parties to pizza nights, pop-ups, and shared tables — built on simple ingredients, slow craftsmanship, and a few pedal-powered ideas in the making.
