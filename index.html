Our Story

More Than Just Pizza

Ni Tinnu started as a simple idea between friends — good pizza, cycling rides and a place to belong. Everything we do is rooted in community.

We pop up in different corners of Bangalore, serve fresh wood-fired pizzas, and create memories that last longer than a meal.

Come for the pizza, stay for the people.
